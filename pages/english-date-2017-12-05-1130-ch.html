每日英语

If you saw the darkness in front of you, don't be afraid, that's because sunshine is at your back

如果你看到前面的阴影,别怕,那是因为你背后有阳光

Life is not a rehearsal, there is no replay button.

人生不是彩排,没有重播键

Only in the dictionary, will success appear before work

只有在字典中,成功才出现在工作之前

Use your smile to change the world,Don't let the world change your smile.

用你的笑容去改变世界,别让世界改变了你的笑容.

It's not about how badly you want something.It's about what you are capable of.

光有志向是不够的,重要的是你的能力.

The important thing in life is to have great aim , and the determination to attain it

人生最终要的就是树立伟大的目标并且实现它.

Don't hide, don't be afraid of the dark, follow the lights,then you can find tomorrow.

不要躲避,不要害怕黑暗,向着太阳你就能找到明天!<疯狂原始人>

Success will belong to those who never say "impossible"

成功属于那些从不说"不可能"的人
